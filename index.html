Explore Programs Join Us

YADA-EXPERIENCE helps young people discover their untapped potential. We create experiences that guide you toward understanding your unique purpose and provide the tools to develop your talents with confidence.

"YADA" comes from a Hebrew word meaning "to know" - to have a close relationship with yourself and your abilities. When you understand your purpose, you unlock a life of impact.

About Us

Our Story

YADA-EXPERIENCE bridges the gap for young people navigating life without clear purpose. We create transformative experiences that help youth uncover their authentic selves through meaningful experiences, supportive relationships, and guided reflection.

Vision

To raise a generation of purpose-driven young individuals who transform their communities through their unique gifts and talents.

Mission

To empower youth to discover their purpose, harness their strengths, and create lasting impact through experiences and mentorship.
