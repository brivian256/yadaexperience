About Us

Discover who we are and why we're passionate about youth purpose development

Our Story

YADA-EXPERIENCE was born from a simple observation: too many young people navigate life without clear direction or purpose. We set out to change that by creating experiences that help youth discover who they truly are.

"YADA" comes from a Hebrew word meaning "to know" - to have a close relationship with yourself and your abilities. This perfectly captures our mission: helping you gain deep knowledge of yourself while providing guidance to bring those abilities to life.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of purpose-driven young individuals who transform their communities through their unique gifts and talents.

Our Mission

To empower youth to discover their purpose, harness their strengths, and create lasting impact through experiences and mentorship.

Our Approach

We believe purpose discovery happens through meaningful experiences, supportive relationships, and guided reflection.

Self-Discovery

Activities that help youth uncover their unique strengths, talents, gifts, and passions.

Skill Development

Hands-on training in communication, leadership, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence.

Connection

Opportunities to connect with peers, mentors, and professionals who share your passion.

Our Objectives

  • Provide actionable steps toward discovering purpose and achieving goals
  • Foster confidence and responsibility in personal and professional journeys
  • Develop leadership and essential life skills
  • Provide mentorship for informed career decisions
  • Create networking opportunities with mentors and professionals
  • Inspire positive community contributions and lasting legacy

Expected Outcomes

Clarity & Direction

Gain clarity about your strengths, purpose, and career paths.

Increased Productivity

Make decisions aligned with your values and purpose for greater efficiency.

Enhanced Well-Being

Experience lower stress and greater fulfillment through purposeful living.
