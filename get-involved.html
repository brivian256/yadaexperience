Get Involved

Join our mission to empower youth to discover their purpose

Join Our Community

There are many ways to contribute to YADA-EXPERIENCE's mission. Whether you have time, skills, or resources to share, you can make a difference in young lives.

Volunteer With Us

Share your time and talents to support our programs and make a direct impact on young lives.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Event Support: Help organize and run workshops and events
  • Program Assistance: Support facilitators during youth programs
  • Administrative Support: Help with office tasks and organization
  • Marketing & Communications: Assist with social media and content creation

Benefits of Volunteering:

  • Make a direct impact on youth development
  • Gain experience in youth work and program delivery
  • Develop new skills and expand your network
  • Receive volunteer training and support
Apply to Volunteer

Become a Mentor

Guide a young person on their purpose journey through one-on-one mentorship.

Mentor Requirements:

  • 21+ years of age
  • Commitment of 4-6 hours per month for at least 6 months
  • Passion for youth development and purpose discovery
  • Willingness to complete mentor training

What Mentors Do:

  • Meet regularly with mentees (in-person or virtually)
  • Provide guidance, support, and accountability
  • Share experiences and insights
  • Help mentees set and achieve goals
Apply to Mentor

Partner With Us

Collaborate with YADA-EXPERIENCE to expand our reach and impact in your community.

Partnership Opportunities:

  • Schools: Bring our programs to your students
  • Community Organizations: Co-host events and workshops
  • Businesses: Sponsor programs or provide internships
  • Faith Communities: Host purpose discovery programs

Benefits of Partnership:

  • Access to proven youth development programs
  • Support from experienced facilitators
  • Customized approach to meet your community's needs
  • Positive impact on youth in your community
