Your donation empowers youth to discover their purpose and create lasting impact

Support Youth Purpose Discovery

Your donation directly empowers young people to discover their purpose and develop skills for meaningful impact. We rely on supporters who believe in the power of purpose-driven living to help make this journey accessible to more youth.

When you contribute, you're investing in the future of young individuals who will shape our communities for decades to come.

Your Impact

What Your Donation Provides

$50

$100

Stories of Impact

Sarah's Journey

Before YADA, Sarah struggled to find direction after secondary school. Through our programs, she discovered her passion for community health education.

Now she leads youth health initiatives in her community. "YADA didn't just help me choose a careerâ€”it helped me understand why I'm here."

Michael's Transformation

As a quiet student often overlooked, Michael hadn't recognized his natural leadership abilities. Through our in-school program, he discovered his gift for bringing people together.

He started a community service club that now engages over 100 students in local projects. "YADA helped me see strengths I didn't know existed."

Amina's Discovery

Feeling pressure to pursue a conventional career despite her creative interests, Amina was anxious about her future. Through our career exploration program, she connected with creative professionals.

Today, she runs a small design business while pursuing education. "YADA showed me I didn't have to choose between passion and making a living."

Our Commitment to You

Donation Policy

YADA-EXPERIENCE is committed to ethical fundraising and transparent stewardship. We pledge that:

  • A minimum of 80% of donations directly fund program activities
  • Administrative costs are kept as low as possible
  • All donors receive acknowledgment and impact updates
  • Donor information is kept confidential

Financial Accountability

As a registered nonprofit, we maintain strict financial controls and undergo regular audits. Our annual reports and financial statements are available upon request.

Recognition

We recognize donors in our annual report, on our donor wall, and through named scholarship opportunities. We respect the privacy of donors who prefer to remain anonymous.
