Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for a program?

You can register for our programs through the Events page on our website. Select the program you're interested in and complete the registration form. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us directly.

Do you offer scholarships for your programs?

Yes! We believe everyone should have access to purpose discovery. We offer scholarships based on need. When registering for a program, indicate that you're interested in scholarship support, and we'll follow up with you.

How can schools partner with YADA-EXPERIENCE?

We offer several partnership models for schools, from one-time workshops to integrated curriculum programs. Contact us through this page or call directly to discuss how we can bring purpose discovery to your students.

What age groups do your programs serve?

Our programs primarily serve youth ages 14-24, with different programs tailored to specific age ranges. We have specialized content for secondary school students, university students, and young adults starting their careers.
